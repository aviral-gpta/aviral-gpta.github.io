Aviral Gupta

I am a masters student in the ECE department of University of California San Diego with a focus on Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control. I am currently a part of the Existential Robotics Lab under Prof. Nikolay Atanasov and am currently working on adaptive control for quadrotors using Neural Differential Equations. I received my Bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics from IIT Delhi, where I was a part of the Formula Student team and worked with various professors on embedded systems, computer vision and control.

My goal is to contribute to the development of autonomous and safe mobile robots. My research interests lie in localization, modelling and control of robots.
